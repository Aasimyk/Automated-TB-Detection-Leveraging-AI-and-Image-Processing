About the Research

This project introduces an automated system for detecting pulmonary tuberculosis (TB) using chest X-ray images (CXR). Under the guidance of Dr. Ramesh Poonia, our research aims to enhance diagnostic accuracy using image processing techniques.

Methodology

  • Preprocessing: Wiener filtering and histogram equalization
  • Registration: Rigid alignment for accurate lung model matching
  • Segmentation: Watershed-based segmentation of lung regions

Datasets Used

  • JSRT Dataset: 247 chest X-rays, including nodule and non-nodule cases
  • Montgomery County Dataset: 138 chest X-rays, annotated for TB
